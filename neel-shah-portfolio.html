About Me

Developer.
Advocate.
Community Builder.

I'm a DevOps engineer with a passion for building communities and making cloud-native technology accessible to everyone. Based in Ahmedabad, India, I organize four community groups — CNCF Gandhinagar, HashiCorp User Group Gandhinagar, Google Cloud Gandhinagar, and Open Source Weekend.

As Developer Advocate at StackGen, I bridge the gap between engineering teams and the developer ecosystem. I've spoken at 40+ conferences globally — including KubeCon India, PlatformCon, HashiTalks, and LinuxFest Northwest — and mentored 15+ hackathons and open source programs.
